i Survival Analysis Project at McMaster University

Results

Survival analysis was used to infer that people who had one Omicron infection had nine times the risk of becoming infected with Omicron BA.5 during the exposure period compared to those who did not have infections, but having two Omicron infections reduced the risk of future Omicron BA.5 infection.
